PhD in Physical Chemistry | Structural Biophysics & Machine Learning

About

I am working on structural prediction methods for RNA. My graduate school work involved time-resolved X-ray solutuion scattering of biological macromolecules. I used complementary molecular dynamics simulations to provide atomic level insight into the experimentally observed kinetic pathways.

Research Interests

  • Structural biology using SAXS/trSAXS
  • Protein and RNA dynamics and conformational ensembles
  • Molecular dynamics simulations of intrinisically disordered systems
  • Machine learning for structural prediction and ensemble heterogeneity
