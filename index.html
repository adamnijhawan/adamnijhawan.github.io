About

I am working on structural prediction methods for RNA. My graduate school work involved time-resolved X-ray solution scattering of biological macromolecules. I used complementary molecular dynamics simulations to provide atomic level insight into the experimentally observed kinetic pathways. I am a lifelong Arsenal supporter; be sure to check out my Arsenal blog
