About

I study the structure and dynamics of biomolecules using small-angle X-ray scattering, time-resolved spectroscopy, and molecular simulations. My current work focuses on structural ensemble modeling and generative ML approaches to explore protein and RNA folding.

Research Interests

  • Structural biology and SAXS/SANS
  • Protein and RNA dynamics
  • Molecular dynamics simulations
  • Machine learning for molecular design
